Hunter Ascend Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Hunter Ascend is a fitness and self-improvement application designed to help users track goals, fitness progress, challenges, nutrition, calorie intake, duels, rankings, and personal achievements.

Information We Collect

  • Google account information used for sign-in.
  • User profile information such as display name and profile picture.
  • Fitness progress, quests, streaks, XP, rankings, duel information, and activity data.
  • Nutrition logs and calorie tracking entries.
  • Optional food photos submitted for calorie analysis.

AI Food Analysis

Hunter Ascend uses third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) services, including Google Gemini and Groq, to analyze food descriptions and food images for calorie and nutrition estimation.

Food images submitted for analysis are processed only for generating nutrition estimates. Food images are not permanently stored by Hunter Ascend after analysis.

Nutrition information generated by AI is an estimate only and should not be considered medical, dietary, health, or professional advice.

How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality and account management.
  • Maintain user accounts and authentication.
  • Display rankings, duels, quests, maps, and fitness progress.
  • Support calorie tracking and nutrition analysis.
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or misuse of the service.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
  • Google Gemini AI
  • Groq AI Services

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms of service.

Data Security

User data is transmitted using encrypted connections. We take reasonable measures to protect stored information; however, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Data Retention

User data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality and comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting us using the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Hunter Ascend is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised update date.
