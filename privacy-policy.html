Hunter Ascend Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Hunter Ascend is a fitness and self-improvement application designed to help users track goals, fitness progress, challenges, nutrition, and personal achievements.

Information We Collect

We may collect and store:

  • Google account information used for sign-in.
  • User profile information such as display name and profile picture.
  • Fitness progress, quests, streaks, XP, rankings, and duel information.
  • Nutrition logs and calorie tracking entries.
  • Optional food photos submitted for calorie analysis.

AI Food Analysis

Hunter Ascend uses third-party artificial intelligence (AI) services to analyze food descriptions and food images for calorie and nutrition estimation.

Food images submitted for analysis are processed by AI providers and are used only to generate nutritional estimates. Food images are not permanently stored by Hunter Ascend after analysis.

Nutrition results are estimates only and should not be considered medical, dietary, health, or professional advice.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide app functionality and account management.
  • Maintain user accounts and authentication.
  • Display rankings, duels, quests, and fitness progress.
  • Support calorie tracking and nutrition analysis.
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or misuse of the service.

Third-Party Services

Hunter Ascend may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
  • Google Gemini AI
  • Groq AI Services

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Security

User data is transmitted using encrypted connections. We take reasonable measures to protect stored information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide app functionality and comply with legal obligations.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting us using the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Hunter Ascend is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
